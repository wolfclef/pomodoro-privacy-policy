# Privacy Policy — Pomodoro Focus Timer

Last updated: 2026-02-21

## Overview
Pomodoro Focus Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect any personal data.

## Local Storage
The app stores only your preferences (e.g., dark/light mode) locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

## Permissions
- **VIBRATE**: Used solely to alert you when you touch the screen during a focus session. No data is collected.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
